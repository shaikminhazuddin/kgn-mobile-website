Who We Are

Your Trusted Mobile Repair Partner

KGN Sanjari Taj Mobiles has been serving the Vizianagaram community for over a decade. We specialize in providing fast, reliable, and affordable mobile repair services with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our team of certified technicians uses only genuine spare parts and the latest diagnostic tools to ensure your device receives the best care possible. We understand how important your mobile device is to your daily life, which is why we strive to provide same-day service for most repairs.

From simple screen replacements to complex motherboard repairs, we handle it all. Our expertise covers all major brands including Apple, Samsung, Xiaomi, Oppo, Vivo, OnePlus, and many more.
